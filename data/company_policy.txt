Acme Corp allows all full-time employees to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Managers must approve remote work schedules.

Expectations:
- Employees must be available online from 9 AM to 5 PM.
- Video meetings should be attended from a quiet, professional environment.
- All work tools (Slack, Jira, GitHub) should be actively used.

Violation of this policy may lead to performance review.
